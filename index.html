— Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions About Working With Us.

What does NWS Media do?
NWS Media is a revenue infrastructure and market positioning agency. We build the technology, automations, content, and ads that fill your pipeline — organized into two branches: Infrastructure (web dev, SEO, AI agents, lead gen, consulting) and Media (paid ads, organic growth, content production). Everything works as one system.
How is NWS Media different from other agencies?
We're operators, not account managers. Every strategy comes from people who've built systems, run ads, and closed clients themselves. We combine infrastructure and media into one engine — because a great ad with a broken funnel is just an expensive lesson. You work directly with the people building your system.
What industries do you serve?
We serve six verticals across NY, NJ & CT: contractors, dentists, med spas, attorneys, CPAs & accountants, and solar companies. We specialize in these industries because focus drives results — we know your market, your customers, and your competition.
How much does it cost to work with NWS Media?
Every engagement starts with a qualification call. Pricing is scoped to your market, your goals, and your growth ceiling. Our Startup tier begins from $3,500, Growth from $8,000, Scale from $15,000, and Enterprise is custom-priced for retainer-based engagements. Ad spend is not included in management fees.
How do I get started?
Fill out our qualification form. We review every application and reach out within 24 hours if it's a fit. From there, we schedule a strategy call to diagnose your current digital presence, identify revenue leaks, and map out exactly what needs to change. Not every business is ready for NWS Media — we're selective because our results come from focus.
Do you handle both website development and advertising?
Yes — that's exactly what sets us apart. Infrastructure without media is a ghost town. Media without infrastructure is noise. We build both and make them work as one compounding system. From custom Next.js websites and AI voice agents to Meta/Google ads and organic growth strategy, it all connects.
